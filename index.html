Attributes (not ordered by rarity)


Tape/Vinyl

The 36 rarest/most popular albums will be represented as a Vinyl Tape. The rest will be mixtapes.

Background Color

The background colors come in: Clouds, Blue, Red, Lavender, Green, Music Notes.

Genre

The genre associated with the album. Genres include: Rock, Rap, EDM, Country, Pop.

Shadow

The design that is shown behind the tape. Shadows include: Dots, Stars, Music Notes, Sound Equalizer.

Body Color + Tapes

The color of the body of the tape. Body colors include: Charcoal, Red, Blue, Lavender, Green.

Wrap Design

The color or design of the wrap on the tape. Wrap designs include: Gold, Green/Blue Gradient, Red/Orange Gradient, Lavender, 80s.

Label (Album Title)

The name of the album. There is only 1 of each album title. The albums were picked from the top music charts in popularity. These are well known albums from some of the top artists.

Stickers

The stickers that correspond with the artist. Some artists might have more than 1 album in the collection, but will have the same sticker. A sticker directory will be posted soon.

FAQs

What is an NFTape?
NFTapes is a collection of 600-700 NFTs that serves as an artistic representation of the most popular music and albums ever released. We are not in any way affiliated with any professional musicians or musical artists. The artist names of the album are not on the art because we would rather expose people to new music they might not have heard of by exploring different styles outside of their comfort zone. Also because of copyright haha. Each NFTape represents a popular album released by some of the most pupular musicians of our time. There are only 1 of each album. The stickers correspond to the artist who released the album, and will be available to identify once we release the sticker chart here on the website after launch. The metadata will also include a link to the album and include the artist/band/producer name.
When and where can I mint an NFTape? How much will it cost to mint an NFTape?
You can mint an NFTape through the fair mint process used by Metaplex Candy Machine by visiting the minting app which we will release at launch. It will cost 2 SOL per NFTape, 1 tape per transaction. The reason for the high price is due to the low supply.

How were NFTapes created?
NFTapes in their attributes were designed by the creators and drawn by the artist. Then the layers were combined to generate each truly unique tape. Each album was handpicked along with the stickers that correspond to each artist. Every attribute was creatively and thoughtfully picked by the team in order to produce a unique piece of art.
How will rarity/rankings for NFTapes work?
Well, music is different to everyone, and people have different music tastes/preferences. We do still have various attributes that will contribute to rarity and ranks such as the shadow, background color, genre, tape wrap, and such. Plus, the Vinyls are the rarest of the collection, representing the most popular albums. As far as the different albums go, the ranking is up to the holders and the communities. Your favorite album might be someone else's least favorite. The value is up to YOU.
